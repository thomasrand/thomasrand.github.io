Services

Steel Fabrication

Our firm fabricates all types of structural steel and miscellaneous metals in conformance with AISC requirements through a shop fabrication assembly process that entails welding, binding, riveting, and fastening of steel components. AISC Certified.

Steel Erection

Our Ironworkers assemble and erect the structural steel framework for single and multi-story buildings, stadiums, arenas, hospitals, towers, and airports in accordance with engineering drawings and AISC standards to the complete satisfaction of our clients.

Engineering & Design Services

IK Construction ensures that the required shop fabrication drawings for steel erection are accurately prepared through a process called steel detailing. Our steel detailer prepares accurate drawings as a basis for shop fabrication & erection of all types of steel members, such as columns, beams, braces, trusses, stairs, handrails, joists, and metal decking.

Structural Renovation & Retrofitting

IK Construction has substantial experience in disassembling & retrofitting of existing steel structures. We assess and diagnose common problems with structural integrity of buildings, as well as strengthening & repairing any existing structural damages.

Safety & Quality

Our staff has over 20 years of experience in all aspects of shop safety, rigging, hoisting, man lift and forklift operations. IK Construction knows that safety is of upmost importance during field activity. We also understand the value of quality control and quality assurance. Our company quality management system procedures are constantly being updated to reflect new industry equipment technology and the latest ISO & AISC standards.

In-Process Inspection & Testing

IK Construction maintains the highest quality of our steel components throughout the shop fabrication process by performing intermediary inspection and testing of steel components based on ISO & AISC criteria and applicable codes and regulations.

Final Inspection & Testing

Once the steel members or components are fabricated at their final phase, we proceed with a final inspection and testing of all steel members to ensure that the steel is fabricated in total accordance with AISC criteria and approved shop drawing configurations.

Process Controls

IK Construction delivers high quality steel structures to our clients due to a rigorous seamless coordination among our shop fabrication assembly workers, our project managers and our field quality control managers.

Site Installation

IK Construction understands the value of establishing continuity of our quality management system processes from our shop to the job site. Our field personnel are trained to follow quality management system guidelines to help ensure that all steel structures are installed according to the highest quality expectations of our clients.

New Jersey Devils Arena (Prudential Center)

IK Construction was awarded this project that entailed two iconic glass and steel cylinders. IKC developed detailed shop drawings that were consistent with design configurations. Our shop drawings included hundreds of welded and bolted unique connections. IKC fabricated all the structural steel members in our own fabrication shop facility. Fabricated structural members of the iconic glass & steel cylinders were then delivered to the job site, and subsequently erected on time and per budget by IKC.

Erection for cylindrical Tube members were very difficult to erect due to the need for them to be in a cylindrical shape. During erection, when the sun beam shines on the the members, it takes a different shape. We had to have a full time Surveyor on site to keep shooting the elevation to direct us on the true plumbness. Many structural calculations were necessary to maintain the proper plumbness during erection.

This very complex joint design was never previously associated with any structural design including that of ASIC. This was the first design of its class. Intensive Engineering input was put forth to implement this design and translates this design into reality. I.K. Construction was able to overcome every challenge to make this project a success. This project was featured the best project for 2008 in the McGraw Hill Construction magazine
