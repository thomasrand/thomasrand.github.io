Safety & Process

Our staff has over 20 years of experience in all aspects of shop safety, rigging, hoisting, manlift and forklift operational training. We at IK Construction know that safety for the job and our people is of upmost importance. We are constantly promoting, training, and delivering our safety directive. Aspects in our process include:

In-Process Inspection & Testing

We maintain the quality of the steel components throughout the shop fabrication process by performing proper inspection and testing of compents based on ASI criteria and applicable codes and regulations.

Final Inspection & Testing

Once the compoenent is fabricated to its final phase, a final inspection and testing is done of all steel components ensuring the steel is fabricated in total accordance to ASI criteria and approved shop drawing configurations.

Process Controls

Production and installation require seamless coordination between project management and superintendent through our field management.

Site Installation

IKC understands that a project is successful only if there is a constant flow of productivity from the shop to the field. Field personnel are trained to follow system guidelines in ensuring that installations are done to conform to approved shop drawing configurations.

Material Quality Assurance

We ensure that all raw materials being purchased conform to contract requirements and material criteria.
